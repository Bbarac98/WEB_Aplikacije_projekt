Croatia wins World Cup bronze medal!


Croatia has won the bronze medal at the football World Cup for the second time in just their 6th appearance. Croatia beat Morocco 2-1 in an entertaining 3rd-4th playoff match at the Khalifa International Stadium on Saturday to finish the 2022 FIFA World Cup on a high. Goals to World Cup sensation Joško Gvardiol and a typical Mislav Oršić finish was enough to give Croatia the win to see them finish third at the World Cup.
